Welcome to the Flamme Rouge Scoreboard

This is an independent fan project created by a couple of friends who enjoy playing Flamme Rouge. It is not affiliated with or commercially related to the official game. We simply wanted a way to play multi-stage races and keep track of results more easily.

In addition to the standard rules, we have come up with a few extra twists and house rules to make the game even more exciting. You can read about these on the Rules page.

What you'll find on the Rules page

  • Assistant rider concept
  • Time trial and team time trials
  • “Good day / Bad day” rider condition
  • Points systems for classement, sprints, and mountain sprints
  • ...and more house rules we’ve experimented with

Have fun exploring, and we hope this scoreboard helps bring your Flamme Rouge races to life!
