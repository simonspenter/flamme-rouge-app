Welcome to the Flamme Rouge Scoreboard

This is a fan-built tool made by a couple of friends who love the game.

Why this app?

The main purpose is to let you play stages continuously and run a proper grand tour with clean tracking of GC, sprints, and mountain classifications.

Optional house rules

We’ve written a short set of additional rules—totally optional but recommended for multi-stage play. You’ll find ideas like an assistant rider, time trials (solo & team), good/bad day conditions, and our points systems for classement, sprints, and mountain sprints. Read them on the Rules page.
